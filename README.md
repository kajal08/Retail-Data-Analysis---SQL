### Sales & Transaction Analysis

### Customer Analysis
- Analyzed gender-wise customer distribution
- Identified cities with the highest customer base
- Found customers with more than 10 successful transactions
- Analyzed revenue generated by customers aged between 25 and 35 in the last 30 days

- Identified number of subcategories under specific product categories
- Analyzed product subcategory revenue for male customers

### Returns & Risk Analysis
- Identified product categories with maximum returns in the last 3 months
- Calculated return contribution compared to total sales
